Hygiene & Infection Control Vigilance

We proactively monitor infection control standards at our partner hospitals to ensure a safe and sterile environment for every patient. Our checks include:

  • ✓Proper sterilization of surgical tools and equipment
  • ✓Strict compliance with staff hygiene protocols, including PPE
  • ✓Daily inspections for cleanliness in patient rooms, ICUs, and waiting areas

Our team educates patients and attendants on hand hygiene, mask usage, and environmental cleanliness to minimize infection risks during their stay.

Safe and Sensible Diagnostics

At Medivisor, no diagnostic test or medical procedure is performed unnecessarily. Our diagnostic safety approach includes:

  • ✓Confirming every test and scan with the treating doctor
  • ✓Avoiding redundant or commercially driven tests
  • ✓Using NABL-accredited private labs where appropriate, to ensure affordability without compromising quality

Our medical team explains every test in advance to ensure full patient understanding.

Optimized Admission & ICU Protocols

Hospital stays are only advised when medically justified. We emphasize outpatient care wherever possible.

  • ✓Pre-operative evaluations are done on an OPD basis (no unnecessary overnight stays)
  • ✓ICU beds are used strictly when clinically indicated
  • ✓Early transfer from ICU to ward when stable, reducing risk of hospital-acquired infections and minimizing costs

This not only enhances safety but significantly reduces the patient's financial burden.

Rapid Emergency Response

Emergencies require speed, clarity, and coordination—and that’s exactly what our system delivers.

  • ✓A dedicated Emergency SOS Group is created for each patient on WhatsApp and Viber. This includes the patient, their attendant(s), Medivisor doctors, and support team—active 24/7.
  • ✓All patient accommodations are booked within 500 meters of both the treating hospital and our Medivisor office.
  • ✓Our Medivisor office is staffed with a round-the-clock paramedic team, trained to respond within minutes. In case the hospital's internal emergency response is delayed (20–30 minutes), our team bridges the gap to stabilize the patient.

This layered emergency system offers real-time human assistance, not just automated systems.

Continuous Medical Oversight

Every patient case is continuously supervised by our in-house clinical team, who:

  • ✓Coordinate and communicate with treating specialists
  • ✓Review test results, prescriptions, and case notes
  • ✓Offer a second medical opinion whenever required

This dual-layer monitoring ensures that potential errors or oversights are caught early and addressed swiftly.

Daily Nurse Visits at Accommodation

Our registered nurses make daily visits to patients’ hotels or apartments to:

  • ✓Ensure medications are taken on time and in the correct dose
  • ✓Monitor vital signs like blood pressure, temperature, and oxygen levels
  • ✓Examine surgical sites for signs of infection or delayed healing

If any abnormal readings are detected, they are escalated to our medical team immediately for clinical intervention.

Medication Review and Verification

Prescription safety is critical. Every medication—before purchase or administration—is checked by Medivisor’s internal doctors for:

  • ✓Correct dosage and timing
  • ✓Harmful interactions between drugs
  • ✓Unnecessary or duplicate prescriptions

We also ensure that all medicines, especially during hospitalization or after discharge, are verified for expiry date and source—eliminating risks of substandard or counterfeit drugs.

Post-Discharge Monitoring & Support

We remain in touch even after the patient leaves the hospital. Our post-treatment care includes:

  • Coordinated follow-up appointments
  • Daily check-ins through the Emergency SOS Group
  • Emergency access to paramedics and doctors if any issue arises

This approach ensures safe recovery and early detection of complications, even when the patient is no longer under hospital care.

Patient Education & Informed Decision-Making

We actively keep the patient and their family informed at every stage, ensuring they are empowered to make safe, confident choices. Our team provides:

  • Clear explanation of diagnosis, treatment options, and timelines
  • Disclosure of risks, side effects, and alternatives
  • Real-time progress updates on the treatment plan
  • A practical list of Do’s and Don’ts during recovery, diet, movement, and hygiene

This transparent communication enhances trust, safety, and patient satisfaction.
